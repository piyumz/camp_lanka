About Camp Lanka

Our Mission

To provide a comprehensive, easy‑to‑use platform that connects adventure seekers with the most stunning and safe camping locations in Sri Lanka, while promoting sustainable and responsible eco‑tourism.

Our Vision

To be the premier online destination for all outdoor enthusiasts exploring the pearl of the Indian Ocean, ensuring every camper is well‑equipped and fully informed before embarking on their journey.

Why Camp in Sri Lanka?

Diverse Landscapes

From misty highlands to dry‑zone wildlife parks and golden beaches — experience multiple climates within hours.

Rich Biodiversity

Camp near some of the highest densities of leopards and elephants in the world — all within a compact island.

Cultural Heritage

Many campsites are located near ancient ruins and UNESCO World Heritage monuments steeped in 2,500 years of history.
